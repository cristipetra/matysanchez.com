Hangnest

May. 2014

USA & Europe

Visit website

In a nutshell, Hangnest allows groups of travelers to "connect with other groups of travelers" to meet and hangout together in the cities they visit - while traveling. Hangnest's profile on Angellist.

Designed and coded just by myself (front-end and backend).

Hangnest was made using the following technologies: PHP, MongoDB, JavaScript, NodeJS, Google Maps, Facebook API and Windows Azure.

It took me about three weeks to define colors, create the logo and design the user interface.

And almost 10 more weeks to code the back-end and front-end. This was my biggest side project so far.

I love to work from the ground and help build new stuff. That is the reason I like to work with small teams. Giving life to an idea, and see that idea grow is something incredible for me. That's why is worth working hard day after day after day.
